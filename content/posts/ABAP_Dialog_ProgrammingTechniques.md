### ABAP Dialog 编程技术

#### 模块池对象的命名约定：BC410

- SAPMV45A：Program name
- MV45A**F01**：Form include 
- MV45A**O01**：PBO include 
- MV45A**I01**：PAI include 
- MV45A**TOP**：Data declaration 
- SAPZNAME：User defined Program 
- ZNAMEF01：User defined Form include
